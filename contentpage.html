Effects of girl child beading

What are the effects of girl child beading on the girl's life?

---Teenage pregnancies -there is no use of contraceptives and most of the time, beaded girls are at the risk of getting pregnant at an early age.
---Psychological trauma- due to the experience of being raped or having to get pregnant while young, it is most likely that many girls suffer from psychological trauma.
---Illegal adoption-when a beaded girl gives birth, the child is considered an outcast since the moran and the girl are both from the same clan and so baby is given up for adoption without the mother's consent.
---Child rights violation -during beading the girls opinion does not count and no girl choses which moran to bead them.The girls also have sex with the morans against their wishes which is violation of children rights.
---Health and reproductive complications-if a girl happens to become pregnant, the foetus has to be aborted and this is through cruel traditional methods of abortion.This might lead to health complications or sometimes can damage the girl's womb or cervix.
---Sexual exploitation-the girls are usually beaded when they are very young and they are forced to have sex with the morans.

What can be done?

  • There is a lot that can be done to save the girl child from sexual exploitation.Girls and women who live in pastoral communities can be educated to create change in their communities for gender equality.
  • Elders and leaders in these communities can promote dialogue that will protect and secure human rights and especially girls' and women's  rights.
  • Speak up against the harmful silent practices on girls
  • Mothers should speak up and defend their girls from being beaded.
